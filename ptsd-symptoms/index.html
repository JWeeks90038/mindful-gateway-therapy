PTSD Symptoms

PTSD symptoms can include intrusive thoughts, flashbacks, nightmares, avoidance, hypervigilance, irritability, sleep problems, emotional numbing, and feeling disconnected from other people or from daily life. Some symptoms are obvious. Others are easier to misread as anxiety, burnout, or mood issues.

Recognizing the symptom pattern can make it easier to seek the right type of support.

FAQ

Are flashbacks always visual?

No. Flashbacks can be emotional, physical, sensory, or body-based without looking like a literal movie replay.

Can PTSD symptoms come and go?

Yes. Symptoms can intensify during stress, anniversaries, life changes, or exposure to triggers.

Get Help Understanding Trauma Symptoms

PTSD symptoms can feel confusing and exhausting. Therapy can help make them more understandable and more manageable.
