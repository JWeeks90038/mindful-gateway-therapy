California PTSD Therapy

PTSD can affect sleep, relationships, work, concentration, and your overall sense of safety. Mindful Gateway Therapy offers online PTSD therapy in California for people experiencing intrusive memories, flashbacks, avoidance, hypervigilance, or emotional numbness after traumatic experiences.

Therapy can help you understand what your body and mind are responding to, build practical regulation skills, and explore whether trauma-focused approaches like EMDR may be a fit.

FAQ

Can online PTSD therapy work?

Yes. Many people find online sessions more accessible and easier to maintain, especially when symptoms make travel harder.

When should someone seek help for PTSD?

It may be time to seek support when symptoms are affecting functioning, relationships, sleep, or your ability to feel safe and present.

PTSD Support Across California

You do not have to keep navigating trauma symptoms alone. Online therapy can provide a more structured path toward recovery.
