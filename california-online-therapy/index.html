California Online Therapy

Online therapy in California gives clients access to consistent, private support without the burden of commuting. Mindful Gateway Therapy offers virtual counseling for grief, trauma, PTSD, anxiety, and emotionally difficult transitions across the state.

For many people, online care increases access and follow-through. When sessions fit real life, therapy becomes easier to sustain.

FAQ

Is online therapy private?

Yes, telehealth sessions are designed to be secure and confidential when clients join from a private setting.

Who can access this service?

California residents can access online therapy while located in California at the time of service.

Accessible Support Across California

If you want therapy that fits your schedule and location, online care may be the right next step.
