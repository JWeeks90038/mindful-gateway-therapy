Meet Sandy Karina Weeks, LPCC

Mindful Gateway Therapy
Online therapy across California

Sandy Karina Weeks, LPCC is the therapist behind Mindful Gateway Therapy. She provides compassionate, evidence-based online counseling for adults navigating grief, trauma, PTSD symptoms, anxiety, emotional overwhelm, and major life transitions throughout California.

Her work is grounded in the belief that therapy should feel both clinically informed and deeply human. Clients often seek support when they feel stuck, exhausted, emotionally flooded, disconnected, or unsure how to move forward after painful experiences. Sandy offers a steady, supportive space to understand those patterns and begin healing with greater clarity and self-trust.

Professional Biography

Sandy Karina Weeks, LPCC works with people who want therapy that is compassionate, practical, and responsive to the realities of their lives. Her approach supports clients dealing with unresolved loss, traumatic experiences, persistent anxiety, post-traumatic symptoms, and the emotional impact of major transitions. Online sessions make it possible to receive care with privacy, continuity, and flexibility from home.

At Mindful Gateway Therapy, Sandy helps clients slow down enough to understand what they are carrying, identify patterns that no longer serve them, and build steadier ways to cope. The focus is not just short-term relief, but meaningful, sustainable change that respects each person’s history and goals.

Services Offered

  • Online grief therapy for bereavement, complicated grief, and difficult life transitions.
  • Trauma therapy for overwhelming experiences, nervous system dysregulation, and recovery support.
  • PTSD therapy for intrusive symptoms, hypervigilance, avoidance, and emotional reactivity.
  • EMDR-related support for clients exploring evidence-based trauma treatment options.
  • Anxiety therapy for chronic worry, panic, overthinking, and stress-related patterns.

Areas of Specialization

Sandy Karina Weeks, LPCC specializes in grief and loss, trauma recovery, PTSD, anxiety, EMDR-related care, and online therapy for California residents who want accessible, compassionate support.

Her practice often serves people looking for a therapist who can help them work through emotionally complex experiences without pressure, judgment, or one-size-fits-all advice.
