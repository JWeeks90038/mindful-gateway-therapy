California Trauma Therapy

Trauma can keep the body and mind reacting long after an experience is over. Online trauma therapy in California can help people understand why they feel constantly on edge, shut down, emotionally numb, or intensely reactive. Mindful Gateway Therapy provides a supportive space to make sense of trauma responses and develop steadier ways of coping.

Therapy often begins with safety and stabilization. That can include learning grounding skills, understanding triggers, and building a clearer sense of how trauma is affecting your relationships, sleep, stress tolerance, and emotional patterns.

FAQ

What does trauma therapy help with?

Trauma therapy can help with hypervigilance, shutdown, panic, avoidance, emotional overwhelm, and long-standing survival patterns.

Can online trauma therapy be effective?

Yes. Many clients benefit from online sessions because they can work from a more familiar and regulated environment.
